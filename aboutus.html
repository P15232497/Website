About Us







Who Are We?



We are the critics that you can trust, bring fair reviews for all different films within the industry. If there's a new film coming out in the cinema but you aren't quite sure if its worth watching, don't fear we review all the new movies at the pictures to make it a little easier to choose. Whether you like blockbusters, indie films or classics we have you covered.





Where I watch the films?



We are currently based in Loughborough, Leicestershire and i have a memebership with Cineworld. we normally go and watch the films we review at the local Cineworld cinema in Loughborough. With fantastic screens and seating arrangement i reccommend my followers to pop in for a film if nearby.
